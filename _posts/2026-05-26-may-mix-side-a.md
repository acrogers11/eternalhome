hello everyone, how are you all doing? at the moment i am eating black rice and chickpeas, wrapping this mix up. having done several interesting things this month, i've drafted and picked at a few posts, but haven't finished very much. ambitiously planning is all fine and dandy, but doing the work and delivering is a little bit harder. need to seek out way more produce to sample this month lol...

this side of this month's tracks lean jaunty, sometimes melodramatic, a little country, a little rock'n'roll. these are always fun to compile, and many tracks were considered that i ultimately nixed. that said, we have a set of VERY special guest mixes coming next month, so be on the lookout for that. june will be glorious! but for now let's appreciate the present. 

peace and love to all you baddies out there.
