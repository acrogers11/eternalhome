how is everyone's april going so far? sarah and i are about to embark on a trip to new mexico, so we'll be posting about that soon enough. we're also sitting on some photos of a trip we took to the tomales point stone lines, so expect that soon, too. in the meantime, enjoy the mix.

the inspiration for this one was noticing that i've been tending toward soft rock, easy listening, and generally peaceful-sounding music for many, many months now, and wanted to make an effort to feature some more ecelectic styles. it was nice to check new releases from labels i haven't listened to in years, and to get some old faves back in the rotation. all that said... mellowness seems to persist.
